Endfair

These EndFair posters are a concept project I worked on for a yearly event hosted in the dorms at Western Washington University. This student-led music festival promotes local artists, giving students a fun experience to take a break from their long weeks of classes.

For this project, I wanted to capture the fun summer energy of the festival and the bands that perform at it. Bellingham has such a beautiful and energetic music scene that I wanted to portray through this collection of posters. Using Illustrator, I combined both shape and type into one bold design.
