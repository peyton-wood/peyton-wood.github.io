EndFair

Event Poster

This is a series of poster concepts I created inspired by the EndFair music festival that is hosted on Western Washington University's campus every year. I utilized bright shapes and fun colors to capture the exciting and youthful energy of the festival. The varying text weights and styles create an interesting texture on this very type-heavy poster.
