Bearly Awake

Brand Concept

I designed a logo and brand identity for Bearly Awake, a coffee shop opening in my hometown. The owners wanted a playful and welcoming feel, so I created a bright color palette and a repeating pattern for merch and branding. The logo features a tired brown bear to tie in with the name and vibe of the shop. I created two versions of the logo for different uses within packaging and merchandise.
