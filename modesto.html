ABOUT/CONTACT GRAPHIC DESIGN

Modesto

This is a type specimen poster I created for the Modesto typeface. After researching the history of the typeface, which was inspired by the local carnivals and train stations in Modesto, I decided to display the font on various vintage tickets. The color palette was inspired by the different ticket examples I was able to find through my research.
