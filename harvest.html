ABOUT/CONTACT GRAPHIC DESIGN

Harvest

This is a brand concept I created for the time tracking brand Harvest. I created a bold and bright brand concept, evolving their current color palette to include a wider variety. I designed a new logo and created some concepts for various brand elements.

Because Harvest is primarily a digital brand, I wanted the brand extensions I created to reflect what the brand would look like on online platforms. I started by creating some concepts for Instagram posts and stories, as well as an app icon and webpage.

For the logo redesign, I wanted to take the “H” of Harvest and make it look like the hands of a clock. As you can see from the logo animation, the cross of the H can swing around, mimicking the movement of a clock’s hands. The hands are highlighted on the logo in a deeper orange color to allow them to really shine. The clock concept was used to represent the time-tracking feature that makes up Harvest’s brand.
