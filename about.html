About me.

Hey there, my name is Peyton, and I am a UI/UX and graphic designer who works to blend my knowledge of business with my creative endeavors. Graduating with a BA in Design and a minor in Business Administration, I've dabbled in a mix of branding, product design, web and app design, illustration, and marketing. I have experience in coding both HTML and CSS, as well as some experience in integrating JavaScript (I coded this portfolio website!). I love collaborating with my peers to bring brands and products to life, bringing together a range of different perspectives into one experience.
