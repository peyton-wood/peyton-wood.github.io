Graphic Design Illustration About

Hi there, I'm Peyton!

I'm a graphic designer and illustrator from Washington State, currently based in Orlando, Florida. I graduated from Western Washington University in 2024 with my BA in Design and a minor in Business Administration.

I try to include a little bit of whimsy in every piece I create. From funky shapes to bright colors, every design should be bold and eye-catching. I currently work as a graphic designer in the cybersecurity sector, with experience across branding, print, web, UI/UX, and illustration.

I'm sure you have questions, set me answer them!

"What tools do you use?"

Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects, Photoshop, Figma, and Procreate.

Bonus: I'm fluent in HTML and CSS, with some knowledge of JavaScript. I coded this portfolio!

"What field of design is your favorite?"

I love them all. But right now branding, print design, and illustration have my heart.

"What is your Tetris high score?"

Currently, it's 226,002. When I'm not designing I love puzzle games, crafting, updating this portfolio, and practicing my harmonica skills (apologies to my neighbors)!
